We are a non-profit organization governed by a volunteer Board of Directors in concert with membership. The Board contracts the City of Medford Parks, Recreation and Facilities Department to provide Senior Center day to day management and to assist with programming. The Medford Senior Center provides services for those 50 and older in a supportive, stimulating and caring atmosphere.
